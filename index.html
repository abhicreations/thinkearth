Our Interests

Remote Sensing & Geographical Information System (GIS)

Geographical Information Science is one of the most smart and advanced subject sought after these days and is used in disguise in each and every field of research and computing both in academic and industries. GIS has a firm demand and controls the following fields of research:

  • Hydrology & Water Resources
  • Geology & Applied Geology
  • Applied Geophysics
  • Information Science/System
  • Applied Electronics
  • Geography
We aim at improving and improvising the sense of understanding and reliability of modern students and teachers who should be free of any illusion. Thus knowledge of GIS at its best sense is essential, which we intend to impart. Our projects aim at bringing about the change required for a new order of knowledge and information science which must be constructive and better for the world.

Computer Science (Engineering) & Information Technology

From space applications to medical advancements, computational tools provide the support and insight to optimize the productivity. The ubiquitous nature of computer science and engineering makes it an obvious extension of theoretical mathematics as an essential tool for research in the following fields but not limited to:

  • Artificial Intelligence (ML, RL, CV)
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • Multimedia Processing
  • Information Retrieval and Storage
  • Digital Forensics
  • Information Security and Cryptography
  • Bio-informatics
Our aim is to prepare scholars to face the challenges offered by this dynamic world and apply their knowledge to solve real world problems. Once equipped with the right tools, they can collaborate with researchers in all fields that use modern computational methods; because tools and intuitive insight will make our scholars stand out in the crowd.
